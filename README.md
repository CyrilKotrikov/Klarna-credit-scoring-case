# Klarna credit scoring case
Klarna credit scoring case by Cyril K

To approach the credit risk prediction task, I experimented with two models:
Logistic Regression – a linear and highly interpretable model widely used in credit risk modelling.
XGBoost – a gradient boosting algorithm that is popular in industry and often outperforms linear models in predictive performance.
Although XGBoost typically provides higher predictive accuracy, I decided to focus primarily on Logistic Regression for the final modelling approach. The reason is that logistic regression requires more structured feature engineering and preprocessing
